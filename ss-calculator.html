▸ How this is calculated ▾ expand
Input — Taxed SS Earnings: Your W-2 Box 3 wages (SS-taxed, capped at each year's SS wage base). Future years are projected estimates.
Step 1 — Index: Past earnings indexed to today's dollars via SSA's Average Wage Index (AWI).
Step 2 — AIME: Top 35 years of indexed earnings ÷ 420 months.
Step 3 — PIA (2025 bend points): 90% of first $1,226 + 32% of next $6,172 + 15% above $7,398.
Step 4 — Claiming age: FRA = 67 (born 1960+). Claiming at 62 reduces benefit up to −30%. Delaying past FRA adds +8%/yr through age 70.
AWI projection: 3.5%/yr (SSA long-range assumption).
