▸ How this is calculated ▾ expand
Input: Your W-2 Box 3 wages (SS-taxed, capped at each year's wage base). Future years are projected.
Step 1 — Index: Past earnings indexed to today's dollars via SSA Average Wage Index (AWI).
Step 2 — AIME: Top 35 years of indexed earnings ÷ 420 months.
Step 3 — PIA (2025): 90% of first $1,226 + 32% of next $6,172 + 15% above $7,398.
Step 4 — Age: FRA = 67 (born 1960+). Early at 62 = up to −30%. Delay to 70 = +24%.
AWI growth: 3.5%/yr projected (SSA long-range assumption).
