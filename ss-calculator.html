How this is calculated:
Step 1 — Index earnings: Each year's wages are indexed to today's wage levels using SSA's Average Wage Index (AWI), capped at the Social Security wage base for that year.
Step 2 — AIME: The top 35 years of indexed earnings are summed and divided by 420 months.
Step 3 — PIA bend points (2025): 90% of first $1,226 + 32% of next $6,172 + 15% above $7,398.
Step 4 — Claiming age adjustments: FRA (Full Retirement Age) is 67 for those born 1960+. Claiming at 62 reduces benefit by up to 30%. Each year past FRA adds 8% (Delayed Retirement Credits) up to age 70.
Future AWI growth: Projected at 3.5%/yr (SSA's long-range assumption). Data is encrypted and saved in your browser.
